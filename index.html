About Steel City HomeCare

Founded in 2023, Steel City HomeCare was created to provide Pittsburgh homeowners with reliable, affordable home maintenance services. We understand that keeping up with home maintenance can be challenging and time-consuming.

Our mission is to take the hassle out of home maintenance so you can focus on what matters most to you. We're committed to providing exceptional service with a focus on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

About Us

Learn more about Steel City HomeCare and our mission

Our Story

Steel City HomeCare was founded by Pittsburgh native Michael Thompson, who saw a need for reliable, affordable home maintenance services in the community. After struggling to keep up with his own home maintenance while working long hours, Michael realized many other homeowners faced the same challenge.

We started with a simple mission: to provide quality home maintenance services that are accessible and affordable for Pittsburgh homeowners. Today, we serve hundreds of satisfied customers throughout the Greater Pittsburgh area.

Our Values

At Steel City HomeCare, we're committed to:

  • Providing exceptional service with attention to detail
  • Being reliable and punctual for all appointments
  • Using quality materials and proven techniques
  • Treating every home with respect and care
  • Building long-term relationships with our customers
